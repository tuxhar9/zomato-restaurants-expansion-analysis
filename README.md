# 🍽️ Zomato Restaurants Expansion Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the Newton School Spreadsheet Project and was successfully accepted after evaluation.

The objective was to analyze Zomato restaurant data and identify the best countries and cities for business expansion using data-driven decision making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Zomato wants to expand into new markets and open new restaurants.

The goal was to identify:

- Countries with lower competition
- High-potential cities
- Customer-preferred cuisines
- Impact of online delivery and table booking
- Pricing strategies for different markets

## 📈 Key Findings

### Online Delivery Impact

- With Delivery: Higher Ratings
- Without Delivery: Lower Ratings

### Table Booking Impact

- Table Booking improves customer satisfaction significantly.
- Restaurants offering booking facilities receive better ratings.

### Pricing vs Ratings

Correlation between pricing and ratings is weak.

**Conclusion:** Better food quality and customer experience matter more than higher prices.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Expand into low-competition markets.
- Launch online delivery from Day 1.
- Provide table booking facilities.
- Use country-specific pricing strategies.
- Focus on highly rated cuisines.
